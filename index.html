Why Choose ICT

My passion for ICT began with a hands-on project: building my first computer. Putting together individual components like the CPU, motherboard, and graphics card to create a fully working system required understanding the complex relationship between hardware and software, and this process really fascinated me. Solving problems when they came up and seeing the system run for the first time gave me an incredible sense of accomplishment. This feeling not only boosted my confidence but also helped me grow and improve my skills. Every success pushed me to take on new challenges. ICT gives me constant opportunities to challenge and improve myself, and because I enjoy overcoming difficulties and the sense of achievement, I believe ICT is the perfect field for me.

Why am I suitable for ICT

Before coming here to study, I had no programming experience or foundation. These two images are my navigation bar code from the first day and fourth day of the PCO course.
