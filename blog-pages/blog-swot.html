SWOT Analysis

Strengths

I have a natural ability to quickly comprehend the structure of knowledge, which enables me to understand new concepts with ease and efficiency.

I excel at time management and effectively organize my daily tasks by utilizing a calendar and reminders. This ensures that I stay on top of my responsibilities and maintain a productive routine.

Weaknesses

I have a fear of public speaking, which often makes me anxious and affects my confidence when addressing large groups.

I feel uncomfortable giving developmental feedback, as I worry about how it might be perceived by others.

Opportunities

My time management and organizational skills ensure productivity and reliability. These qualities may inspire others and improve overall team efficiency.

My sensitivity in expressing feelings and giving feedback fosters a supportive work environment. Although public speaking is challenging for me, it encourages me to find creative ways to share ideas, such as through writing or small group discussions.

Threats

The course load is too heavy, and I am unable to complete assignments in my spare time.

My English skills are not strong, which makes me slow in reading course materials and completing assignments.
